MigiWa Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Overview

MigiWa is a photo and file organizer for iOS and Android. This privacy policy explains how MigiWa handles data when you use the app.

Data MigiWa Does Not Collect

MigiWa itself does not create user accounts, require sign-in, operate a developer server for your content, or upload your photos and files to us. Specifically:

  • We do not store your photos or files on our servers.
  • We do not sell your photos, files, or personal information.
  • We do not use your photos or files for advertising or analytics.

Photo and File Access

MigiWa requests access to photos, videos, and local files so it can display, organize, move, copy, import, export, or delete items according to the actions you choose in the app. This processing happens on your device through iOS or Android system storage APIs. Photo and file contents are not sent to MigiWa servers.

Advertising

MigiWa may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect or receive device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, interaction data, diagnostics, and other data as described by Google in order to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads.

You can manage ad personalization in your device settings. On Android, go to Settings > Privacy > Ads. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.

Learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Purchases

MigiWa offers optional Pro purchases or subscriptions. Payments are processed by the App Store on iOS and Google Play on Android. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Children's Privacy

MigiWa is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support.migiwa@gmail.com.
